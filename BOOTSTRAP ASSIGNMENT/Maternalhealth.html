About the Maternal Health Project

This initiative focuses on reducing maternal mortality and improving access to care. We provide counseling, organize health camps, and engage communities to ensure mothers receive the support they need for safe pregnancies and deliveries.

What We Do

    Counseling:Guidance for mothers on antenatal care, safe delivery practices, and postnatal support.

    Health Camps:Organizing maternal health check-ups, education sessions, and referrals to clinics and hospitals.

    Community Dialogues:Engaging families, leaders, and stakeholders to promote maternal health rights and safe motherhood practices.

    Awareness Campaigns:Educational drives to inform communities about maternal health, nutrition, and safe pregnancy.


Our Impact

Through these activities, we have improved maternal health awareness, supported mothers during pregnancy, and strengthened community support systems. Every effort contributes to safer motherhood and healthier families.
