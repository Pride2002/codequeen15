About the Women's Empowerment Project

This initiative equips women with practical skills and opportunities to thrive. By focusing on tailoring, hairdressing, and catering, we help women gain economic independence, reduce vulnerability, and strengthen their role in community development.

What We Do

    Tailoring Training:Providing hands-on training in tailoring, enabling women to create garments and generate income through their skills.

    Hairdressing Skills:Equipping women with professional hairdressing techniques to open salons or offer services within their communities.

    Catering Training:Training women in catering and hospitality, empowering them to run small businesses or support community events.

    Community engagements: Financial literacy trainings are conducted and these have empowered women by teaching them how to budget, save, and manage money wisely. With these skills, many women have gained confidence, started small businesses, and improved their families’ well-being. Learning about finances has strengthened their independence and opened doors to new opportunities.

Our Impact

Through these programs, women have gained practical skills, started small businesses, and built stronger support networks. The project has transformed lives by reducing vulnerability and promoting independence.
